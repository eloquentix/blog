May I kindly ask to share a new term: RandomMaxxing.

In an age of calendars and AI, RandomMaxxing is the life philosophy that the way to stay human, to experience life as something other than being a peg of varying utility in a low-temperature, computer-driven existence, is to just be and do whatever, as much as possible.

It's the sensible form of the popular Retardmaxxing.
