Having worked on the best VPP in Europe for the last 10 years, what Spain's massive blackout, exposed the risks of relying heavily on renewables without robust grid solutions. Virtual Power Plants, like E.ON's, are a part of the solution. By linking solar, batteries, and smart devices, VPPs balance the grid in real-time, preventing outages. With Spain running 80%+ on renewables pre-blackout, VPPs are critical to stabilize clean energy grids.
