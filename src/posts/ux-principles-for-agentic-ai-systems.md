Emerging UX principles for agentic AI -- systems that act autonomously to achieve goals -- focus on balancing user control, trust, and seamless interaction.

Transparency & Explainability: Users need to understand why an AI makes decisions. Provide clear, concise explanations in natural language.

User Control & Agency: Allow users to set boundaries, override decisions, or adjust goals.

Context-Aware Adaptability: Agentic AI should adapt to user context while avoiding over-personalization.

Proactive Communication: Anticipate user needs and initiate interactions sparingly.

Trust-Building Feedback Loops: Regularly confirm actions and solicit feedback to refine behavior.

Ethical Guardrails: Design interfaces that highlight ethical considerations.

Multimodal Interaction: Support diverse inputs -- voice, text, gestures.

Progressive Disclosure: Start with simple interactions, revealing advanced features as users gain confidence.
