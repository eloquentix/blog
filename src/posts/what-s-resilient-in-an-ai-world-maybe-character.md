Many who are aware of AI capabilities today are scared of their future. What is resilient in an AI world? Skills? Judgement? Rolodex? Knowledge? All fall into the 'not really.' Maybe Character stays.

In capitalist China, it seems there is a brutal battle where some are trying to automate the jobs of their colleagues via skill.md files. That led to the idea of a skill-obfuscation skill: it takes skill.md files and makes them look good but be practically useless. That struck a cord and went viral.

I used Claude to translate the skills from Chinese to English. If you're looking to build some skills yourself, take a look. It may help create the right mindset for skill building.
